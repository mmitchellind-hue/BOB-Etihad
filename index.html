Tips & tricks · the meat of it

How to pull ₹18,000 back

~₹9,000 is straightforward. The other ~₹9,000 comes from turning Reward Shop vouchers into gift cards if you don't need the miles. Follow it in order. You'll want a (free) Etihad account, and a (free) Marriott account if you use the cheapest Gold trigger.

Part A

Getting to Silver & Gold tiers

Excluded categories still count toward the first-transaction, ₹25k and ₹50k milestones — so this part is easy to knock out.

  1. Do your first transaction — anywhere

    Any spend works to trigger the Silver flow (even excluded categories count for these milestones).

  2. Wait for the Silver email (7–10 days)

    You'll get a confirmation that Silver Tier is active.

  3. Choose Reward Shop Voucher as a custom benefit

    Hit “Choose your Custom Benefits” and pick Reward Shop Voucher — this gives you 2 vouchers. Pick any second benefit; it doesn't matter.

  4. Save the 2 voucher codes right away

    They're available immediately. Copy and store them — they disappear once you reach Gold.

  5. Trigger Gold with a small points buy

    Buy the minimum 1,000 Marriott points for $12.5 to trigger Gold — if you're before 5 Aug. Buy Marriott points
    After 5 Aug you'll need to spend ~$104 to trigger it (ideally not on buying points).

  6. Wait for the Gold email (2–4 days)

    Gold Tier confirmation lands within a few days of that transaction.

  7. Choose Reward Shop Voucher again — for 4 vouchers

    At Gold, picking Reward Shop Voucher gives you 4 vouchers. Choose the other 3 benefits based on what you'd actually use flying Etihad.

  8. Save all 4 Gold voucher codes

    They're visible immediately. You now hold 6 voucher codes total — 2 from Silver, 4 from Gold.

Part B

Cashing out the ₹18,000

Now you convert those 6 voucher codes into Flipkart SuperCoin gift cards on the Etihad Reward Shop.

  1. Get at least 401 Etihad miles into your account

    You need a small miles balance to check out. Either complete ₹25k spend in 60 days then wait ~60 more for the first 10k miles — or transfer in from another card (e.g. 500 Axis Edge Miles → 1,000 Etihad Miles in ~20 hours; HDFC and others work too).

  2. Have your 6 voucher codes handy

    2 from Silver + 4 from Gold. Log in at rewards.etihad.com

  3. Open All categories → Gift Cards & Vouchers

  4. Pick the Flipkart SuperCoin Voucher

  5. Choose the ₹500 voucher, quantity 3 → Buy Now

  6. Apply a voucher code → Pay 37 Miles

    Paste your first Reward Shop code, hit Apply Code, and the price drops to a token amount. Then pay.

  7. Repeat for the other 5 codes

    Each order delivers 3 SuperCoin vouchers to your inbox in ~3 days. Across all 6 codes you'll land ₹9,000 of SuperCoin vouchers — usable as 9,000 SuperCoins in Flipkart, or in apps like GoSats

  8. Then decide what to do with the 15,000 miles

    Once conditions are met you'll still have 15,000 Etihad miles. Fly them (India–UAE, India–Sri Lanka on SriLankan, and more), or if you're not flying, repeat the steps above with the slider pushed fully to miles.

    Not flying? Push the slider all the way to miles before Buy Now — you can grab a 5,000-SuperCoin voucher in one go. Build your balance to 17,000 miles and you can buy the 10,000-SuperCoin voucher outright.
